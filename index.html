About Me

My name is Josh Hyatt. I've been programming games and simulations for most of the last 15 years. I love to learn and you'll never find me working on the same thing for more than a few months (fortunately or unfortunately). I've always been interested in aviation and aerospace, and I'm working on getting my private pilot's license. I have the chubbiest 8-month-old I've ever met and a wonderful wife who lets me talk her ear off about whatever my latest project is, even if she doesn't understand a word of it.

Logan, Utah

Logan is a great mix of "close to civilization" (we have Costco) and "hasn't yet grown into a big city". You can drive for 10 minutes in any direction and be in the mountains, a small town frozen in the late 1800s, or in a wide-open field.
